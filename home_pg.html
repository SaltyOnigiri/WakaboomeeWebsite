What Is Wakaboomee?

Wakaboomee is a 501(c)3 that provides STEAM Education in the form of Coding, Chess, Mindfulness, Art, Spanish, Music, Yoga, and so much more to children across the world using zoom. We have paired up with numerous non-profits to provide an engaging curriculum of educational enrichment while giving life lessons that resonate and inspire greatness. Experience the magic of the Wakaboomee Program 501(c)3 today. We can't wait to meet you!

Narkie Nartay

CEO & Founder

Since I was a child, I’ve had a passion for helping people. But for much of my life, helping people was limited and expressed rather perfunctorily. It was only when I wrote a children’s book and volunteered to read it weekly to various groups of students at a Title-1 elementary school that I realized how much a green little creature named Wakaboomee had the ability to impact the lives of children. Together with Wakaboomee and a group of incredible individuals who have a real passion for change.
